From: mirror diameter = eyepiece field stop * exit pupil * 57.3 / (field of view * eyepiece focal length * 25.4)

For example: 21mm Ethos with 36.2mm field stop, coma corrector with 1.15x factor, desired FOV of 1.8 deg and 13.2 inch primary mirror at F3 with focal length = 13*3.2=39.6:
exit pupil = 21/3/1.15=6.1, therefore aperture = 2.256 * 36.2 * 6.1 / 1.8 / 21 = 13.2
